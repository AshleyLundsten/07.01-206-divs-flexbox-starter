Ashley's Adventures in Valencia
Hi, I'm Ashley. I moved to Valencia, Spain over 5 years ago. Since I got here, I've been exploring all the tiny towns, going on beautiful hikes, finding the most gorgeous beaches, and I even joined a Falla. Join me on my adventures, where I will share some of the best stops for your visit to the area and get you acquainted with the world of Fallas, a major part of Valencia's culture.
About Me:
In 2017, I left my job to see more of the world. My first stop was Valencia, Spain. On day 2 of my trip, I met some new friends for tapas, ended up in a club, and gave a random stranger a hug in the late hours of the night, after a few glasses of wine and whiskey.
What happened next:
I guess you could say "the rest is history". But really, I continued wandering around Europe for a few months, meeting people and seeing new cities. My new boo came to visit me in Poland, and then Sevilla. I ended my little whirlwind back in Valencia, where we spent the last two weeks of my pre-retirement together.
Bigger plans:
When I got back to the states, I knew I could NOT go back to traditional office life, and I've been crafting this little bit of freedom ever since. I ended up in a law firm, where I spent 4 years reuniting with old coworkers from abroad. Finally, I decided to listen to my heart and work towards my passion: telling you about travel.
