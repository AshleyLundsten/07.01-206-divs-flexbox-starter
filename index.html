Do you prefer to spend your vacation jumping in the ocean, hiking in the mountains, or lounging by the pool?

Prettiest beach towns in Spain:


  1. Altea: A beautiful white-walled old town, Altea's rocky beach is a beautiful contrast to the blue water.
  2. Calpe: Calpe boasts sandy shores with pretty blue water. Hikers will love the opportunity to climb the PeËœon de Ifach.
  3. Javea: Crystal blue water with mountain views, sandy beach and plenty of restaurants and bars make Javea an amazing spot to spend the day.

Best towns with mountain views near Valencia, Spain:


  1. Requena/Utiel: If you love wineries with a view, this is your place! Just about an hour from Valencia city, you can take wine tours, see underground caves, or visit the quaint historic old town.
  2. Guadalest: Not far from Alicante is the small mountain town of Guadalest. From the town, you can get both mountain and water views, and even look over a rich blue lake. Inside of the town's walls, there are plenty of restaurants, stores, and a museum.
  3. Sella: If you're looking for a moderate level hike to pass the time, Sella has a good number of trails that lead to spectacular mountain top views.
